You're Invited to the Ultimate Halloween Furry Party!

Get ready for a spook-tacular night of fun, friends, and fur at our Halloween-themed furry event in St. Petersburg hosted by Tampa Furry Club! We’ve rented out a cozy lounge just for this event, where you can let loose, dance, and make new friends in a lively and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're a seasoned furry, a first-timer, or just love Halloween, come join the party and celebrate with us in style!
You must be 18 or older to attend.

What’s Happening?

  • Live DJs & Dancing: Groove to an eclectic mix of tunes spun by our DJs, from spine-tingling beats to dance hits that will keep you moving all night.
  • Games & Activities: Jump into fun games, mingle with fellow party-goers, and take part in some activities we've set up.
  • Costume Contest: Bring your A-game! Whether you’re rocking your fursuit, favorite Halloween look, or a creative costume, there’s a chance to win awesome prizes.
  • Special Drinks & Eats: Enjoy a special menu with Halloween-inspired drinks and tasty bites made just for this occasion.

Dress to Impress

Costumes and fursuits are not just encouraged—they’re what make this party! Come as your favorite character, your fursona, or anything spooky and fun. Let’s see those fursuits and costumes light up the night!

  • At the Door: $25 – In case you want to decide last minute.

Why You Don’t Want to Miss This

This isn’t just any party—it’s a furry Halloween celebration! The lounge will be decked out in spooky décor, the vibe will be electric, and you'll be surrounded by people who love to celebrate just like you do. Come for the fun, stay for the memories, and leave with some new friends!
