What the heck is Howl's Eve?

Howl's Eve is a Halloween party hosted by Tampa Furry Club.
You must be 18 or older to attend, and you'll need a ticket (~$20) to get in.

Come enjoy food, music, and Halloween fun with fellow furries in St. Petersburg, Florida!
Fursuits are welcome and encouraged, and there will be a small headless lounge.
